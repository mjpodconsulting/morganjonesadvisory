Services

What I do
and how it works.

I advise podcast networks, platforms, and creators on high-stakes content decisions — bringing clarity, structure, and senior judgment when it matters most. Every engagement is scoped, finite, and focused on real decisions.

01

Portfolio & Slate Strategy

Networks & Platforms

Strategic evaluation of an existing podcast or content slate to clarify priorities, tradeoffs, and investment decisions. The goal is a sharper, more intentional portfolio — one where every show has a clear role.

  • Full slate diagnosis — what's working, stalled, or misaligned
  • Clear role definition for each show (subscriptions, ads, brand, IP, talent)
  • Renewal, pause, sunset, or reframe recommendations
  • 6–12 month portfolio roadmap
Best for

Networks, platforms, or studios needing sharper focus and better outcomes from existing content.

02

Show Pitching & Creative Development

Creators & Producers

High-level creative evaluation and pitch refinement at the decision-making level. I help strong ideas become greenlight-ready — with the discipline and market awareness that gets them bought.

  • Pitch teardown and rebuild
  • Format and franchise architecture
  • Host and talent fit assessment
  • Greenlight readiness and risk analysis
  • Platform- or buyer-specific positioning
Best for

Creators, producers, or companies with strong ideas that need clarity, discipline, and market alignment.

03

Talent & Market Scouting

Networks & Platforms

Independent evaluation of the podcast and creator landscape to identify acquisition, partnership, or expansion opportunities — without the guesswork.

  • Scouting existing shows and emerging talent
  • Buy vs. build vs. partner recommendations
  • Risk and scalability assessment
  • Franchise and integration potential analysis
Best for

Networks or platforms looking to expand their slate intelligently.

04

Go-To-Market & Distribution Strategy

All clients

Strategic design of how a show enters the market and how success is defined. Launching without a distribution strategy is the most common — and most expensive — mistake in podcasting.

  • Launch and windowing strategy
  • Feed, paywall, and platform approach
  • Subscription vs. ad tradeoff analysis
  • KPI definition tied to real decisions
  • Post-launch optimization framework
Best for

Teams launching premium content or recalibrating underperforming releases.

05

Creator & Show Checkup

Independent Creators

A structured, senior-level audit designed to diagnose what's working, what's holding a show back, and what to do next. This is not ongoing coaching — it's a clear-eyed product and strategy assessment.

  • Review of recent episodes and format
  • Positioning and audience clarity assessment
  • Host performance and structural notes
  • Distribution and growth levers
  • Monetization readiness (ads, subs, brand, IP)
  • Written recommendations and prioritized next steps
Best for

Independent creators, journalists, or teams who want expert judgment without a long-term commitment.

06

Interim / "Hired Gun" Leadership

Organizations

Embedded strategic leadership during moments of transition, reset, or rapid build. I step in as acting head of content or strategy, deliver real work, then hand off cleanly.

  • Acting head of content or strategy support
  • Slate or team realignment
  • Executive-level decision support
  • Systems, frameworks, and process installation
  • Clean handoff after engagement ends
Best for

Organizations needing senior judgment without a permanent hire.

About

Morgan
Jones.

Award-winning podcast executive with 15+ years at the intersection of creative judgment and commercial strategy. Former Head of US Originals at Wondery/Amazon.

I spent the better part of 15 years inside some of the most consequential podcast organizations ever built — Audible, Amazon Music, and Wondery — learning what it actually takes to build a slate, develop a show, and make decisions that hold up under pressure.

At Wondery, I built and led the Commissioned Originals team, running a slate of 25–30 shows at a time across every genre while managing a team of 10+ producers and maintaining relationships with 30+ external studios and creators. The work ranged from Ghost Story (Ambies Best Documentary, Apple's first Series Essential, 5M series downloads) to Hysterical (Apple and Time Podcast of the Year 2025, Pulitzer Prize finalist) to complex international co-productions and global launches in four markets.

"He earns trust fast, unsticks blockers, and gets teams rowing in the same direction."

In 2024, I became Head of US Originals Strategy & Programming — overseeing the full lifecycle of Wondery's US slate, leading greenlight processes, owning MBR/QBR reporting for senior leadership, and co-authoring strategic documents for Amazon's S-Team. Simultaneously, I led international content across the UK, Germany, Mexico, and Brazil.

Now I bring that experience to organizations and creators who need senior-level judgment without a full-time hire. Whether that means a slate review, a pitch teardown, a launch strategy, or acting as an embedded thought partner during a critical window — I work at the decision layer, with clear scope and defined outcomes.

I'm based in San Francisco and work with clients remotely and in person.
